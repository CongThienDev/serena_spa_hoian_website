# CONTENT_GUIDE.md — Copywriting Rules

## Voice
Write like a premium wellness host, not a discount advertiser.

## Tone
- Calm
- Elegant
- Sensory
- Clear
- Trustworthy

## CTA Patterns
Primary:
- Book Your Escape
- Book Now
- Reserve Your Treatment
- Start Your Wellness Journey

Secondary:
- Explore Services
- View Treatments
- Discover Wellness
- Read More

## Service Description Pattern
Each service should include:
1. What it is
2. Who it is for
3. Benefits
4. Duration
5. Price
6. Booking CTA

## Example Service Copy
“Serena Signature Massage blends soothing pressure, gentle stretching, and aromatic oils to release tension and restore balance after a long day exploring Hoi An.”

## Avoid
- Too many exclamation marks
- Cheap / sale-heavy wording
- Unsupported medical claims
- Overpromising results
- Keyword stuffing

## Blog Style
Blogs should be helpful and SEO-friendly.
Use:
- Short paragraphs
- Clear H2/H3 headings
- Internal links to services
- Soft booking CTA near the end

## Multilingual Notes
English first for international tourists. Vietnamese and Korean should be localized, not directly machine-translated when possible.
