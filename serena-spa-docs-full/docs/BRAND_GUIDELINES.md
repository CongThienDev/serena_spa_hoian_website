# BRAND_GUIDELINES.md — Serena Spa Hoi An

## Brand Essence
Serena Spa Hoi An is a calm wellness sanctuary where travelers can restore their energy, release stress, and glow naturally.

## Brand Keywords
- Calm
- Premium
- Healing
- Natural
- Elegant
- Soft
- Warm
- Trustworthy
- Feminine
- Serene

## Positioning
A premium yet approachable spa in Hoi An offering relaxation massage, facial care, body therapy, natural essential oils, and curated wellness experiences.

## Tone of Voice
- Gentle
- Reassuring
- Warm
- Professional
- Elegant
- Slightly poetic, but still clear

## Example Copy Tone
Good:
- “Step into a quiet sanctuary in Hoi An.”
- “Release tension, restore balance, and let your natural glow return.”
- “Thoughtfully crafted treatments for body, mind, and soul.”

Avoid:
- “Cheap massage deal today.”
- “Best price guaranteed!!!”
- “Super hot promotion.”
- Overly aggressive sales language.

## Logo Usage
- Keep generous whitespace around the lotus logo.
- Use terracotta or walnut logo on light backgrounds.
- Use white logo on terracotta/peach backgrounds.
- Do not stretch or distort logo.

## Photography Direction
Use:
- Warm light
- Soft shadows
- Linen, towels, wood, candles, flowers
- Interior room shots
- Treatment detail shots

Avoid:
- Harsh fluorescent lighting
- Over-edited saturated colors
- Medical/clinic aesthetic
- Dark black luxury style

## UI Feeling
The website should feel like entering a peaceful spa lobby: warm, clean, slow, soft, and trustworthy.
