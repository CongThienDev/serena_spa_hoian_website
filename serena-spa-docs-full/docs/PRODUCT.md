# PRODUCT.md — Website Product Specification

## Product
Serena Spa Hoi An website for international and Vietnamese tourists looking for premium spa, massage, wellness, facial care, body therapy, and relaxation services in Hoi An.

## Target Users
- Tourists in Hoi An searching for massage/spa.
- Korean, English-speaking, and Vietnamese customers.
- Couples looking for relaxation packages.
- Travelers tired after walking in Ancient Town.
- Customers who prefer quick mobile booking.

## Main Business Goals
- Increase direct bookings.
- Build premium trust before guests arrive.
- Improve Google SEO visibility.
- Support social traffic from Instagram, Naver, Kakao, WhatsApp, Zalo.
- Showcase interior, service quality, and wellness brand identity.

## MVP Pages

## Conversion Funnel
### Social Flow
Instagram / Naver / Facebook → Home → Service Detail → Booking Widget → WhatsApp confirmation

### SEO Flow
Google Search → Blog / Service SEO page → Related service → Booking

### Direct Flow
Google Maps / business card QR → Home → Floating contact → Call / WhatsApp / Zalo

## MVP Booking Requirements
Booking form fields:
- Service
- Duration
- Date
- Time
- Number of people
- Name
- Phone
- Email optional
- Special request optional

Notification target:
- Email or Telegram first.
- Zalo/WhatsApp manual confirmation by staff.
